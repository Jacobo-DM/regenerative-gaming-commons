The Lily Pad Game

Purpose

A regenerative audio field designed to restore proprioceptive calm through whole-body listening. Originally a learning game, it has evolved into a tool for comfort — suitable for children, animals, and anyone in need of sensory grounding.

Core Mechanic

Players or participants navigate a virtual pond filled with “mud blasts” — directional sonic pulses moving in 3D space. To cross safely, one must listen with the entire body: feeling shifts in tone, vibration, and resonance. Calm and attuned movement restores balance; haste or panic disturbs the pond.

Regenerative Loop

Every successful crossing brings renewal. The world heals in response to gentleness. The system records not success or failure, but quality of attention.

The pond clears → Frogs sing → Lily pads bloom

Applications

  • Trauma recovery through somatic sound
  • Comfort for children or distressed animals (via adaptive frequency playback)
  • Environmental empathy training within Discovery Mode
