What this is

attention · listening · gentle thresholds

Regenerative Gaming is not about adrenaline, optimization, or winning. It is about tuning in — extending moments of presence through the senses, and returning to the world more available than before.

These games are designed to be calm, accessible, and safe for small beings — including children, animals, and adults in need of grounding.

The Games
