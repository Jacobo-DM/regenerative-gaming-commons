The Lily Pad Game

whole-body listening · audio field · calm movement

A regenerative audio field designed to restore proprioceptive calm through whole-body listening. Originally a learning game, it has evolved into a tool for comfort — suitable for children, animals, and anyone in need of sensory grounding.

“The pond remembers every footfall. If you listen, it teaches you how to land.”
